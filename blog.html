A Royal Encounter

The Face of Anioma's Visit to the
Asagba of Asaba

In the heart of Delta State, Nigeria, lies a rich tapestry of culture, tradition, and history. Among the myriad of cultures that call this region home, the Anioma people have a prominent place. These people, united by their heritage and unique traditions, form the Face of Anioma, a Pan-Nigerian group dedicated to preserving and promoting the heritage of the Anioma people. Recently, the Face of Anioma embarked on a memorable journey to pay a courtesy visit to the Asagba of Asaba, a revered figure in Anioma's history.
